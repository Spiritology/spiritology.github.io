## Bonus: Ad Creative Direction Notes

### For Variation 1 (Curiosity):
- **Image:** Abstract brain/consciousness visual, layered glass effect, or calm person with eyes closed and subtle light overlays. Clean, premium aesthetic.
- **Video option:** 15-second animation showing the 4 layers of consciousness stacking, ending with "Which layer are you on? Free assessment — link below."

### For Variation 2 (Social Proof):
- **Image:** Real person (or realistic stock that feels authentic), looking directly at camera. Quote overlaid. Stats at bottom.
- **Video option:** 30-60 second testimonial compilation. Real students sharing 1-2 sentence transformation moments. End with assessment CTA.
- **Carousel option:** 3-5 slides, each with a different student quote + photo, final slide = CTA.

### For Variation 3 (Direct Offer):
- **Image:** Clean course mockup or "The Architect" branded visual. Price clearly displayed. Professional, premium feel — not discount/sale aesthetic.
- **Video option:** Dr. Elias Voss speaking directly to camera. 60 seconds. "If you've taken the assessment and you're ready for the next step — here's what I built for you." Personal, warm, direct.

## Campaign Structure Recommendation

| Campaign | Audience | Ad Variation | Objective |
|----------|----------|-------------|-----------|
| Prospecting | Cold (interest + lookalike) | Variation 1 (Curiosity) | Conversions: Assessment Completion |
| Retargeting Tier 1 | Warm (engagers, video viewers) | Variation 2 (Social Proof) | Conversions: Assessment Completion |
| Retargeting Tier 2 | Hot (assessment completers) | Variation 3 (Direct Offer) | Conversions: Purchase |
| Upsell | Past purchasers | Custom (All-Access Pass) | Conversions: Purchase |

**Budget Allocation Suggestion:**
- 60% Prospecting
- 25% Retargeting Tier 1
- 15% Retargeting Tier 2 + Upsell

**KPIs to Track:**
- Cost per assessment completion (target: $3–8)
- Assessment-to-enrollment conversion rate (target: 3–5%)
- Cost per enrollment (target: $80–150 for The Architect)
- Email sequence open rates (target: 40%+ for Email 1, 25%+ for Email 5)
- Return on ad spend (target: 4x+ at scale)
