About

What is Spiritology?

A comprehensive understanding of the technology that has freed millions.

Spiritology is the study and practice of the spirit in relation to itself, other spirits, and the physical universe. It encompasses a body of knowledge and a precise technology for the rehabilitation of the human spirit — restoring to it abilities, certainties, and freedoms that have been lost through accumulated trauma and reactive programming.

The word "Spiritology" derives from the Latin spiritus (spirit, breath of life) and the Greek logos (word, reason, study). It literally means "the study of the spirit" or "knowing about knowing."

Unlike faith-based religions, Spiritology does not ask its practitioners to believe. Rather, it provides tools — precise, systematic, and verifiable — through which each individual can discover truth for themselves. The fundamental discoveries of Spiritology are not articles of faith; they are observable realities that anyone can verify through the technology itself.

Spiritology was founded in 1971 by Dr. Elias Voss, following two decades of research into the nature of consciousness, the architecture of the human mind, and the common principles underlying every tradition of inner transformation throughout human history.

Today, the Church of Spiritology operates in 184 countries, with more than 12,000 churches, missions, and groups serving a worldwide membership of over 4.2 million people.

Doctrine

Core Beliefs

I. The Spirit

The spirit is the individual being — immortal, aware, and capable of infinite creation. It is not a product of the brain, nor a byproduct of matter. It is the source and foundation of consciousness itself. Every human being is a spirit.

II. The Reactive Mind

The reactive mind is a stimulus-response mechanism that records moments of pain, unconsciousness, and loss. These recordings — called imprints — operate below awareness and drive compulsive behavior, irrational fears, psychosomatic illness, and spiritual suppression.

III. Processing

Processing is the central practice of Spiritology — a precise procedure through which imprints are located, examined, and permanently erased from the reactive mind. Processing restores the spirit to self-determined action, clear perception, and full spiritual ability.

IV. The Bridge

The Bridge to Total Freedom is the complete map of spiritual states from human bondage to total liberation. It consists of precisely ordered levels, each building upon the last, forming an exact route that any being can walk to achieve full spiritual freedom.

V. The Spiritual Dynamics

Existence operates through eight fundamental dynamics — drives toward survival across expanding spheres: self, family, groups, humanity, life, the physical universe, the spiritual universe, and infinity. True spiritual health means balanced flourishing across all eight.

VI. Ethics & Integrity

Ethical conduct is not imposed from without but arises naturally as the reactive mind is cleared. A being operating from the spirit — rather than from reactive compulsion — naturally acts in accordance with the highest good across all dynamics.

The Path

The Bridge to Total Freedom

The precise route from spiritual bondage to complete liberation. Each level must be completed in sequence.

I

The Awakening

Recognition of the spirit. First awareness that you are not your body, your thoughts, or your reactive mind. The Spiritual Evaluation maps your starting point. Beginning of foundational study and introductory processing.

II

Clarity

Systematic clearing of surface-level reactive patterns. Restoration of emotional equilibrium, mental clarity, and the ability to perceive without distortion. First experiences of enhanced spiritual perception.

III

Liberation

Deep processing of core reactive programming — the foundational imprints installed in early life and past existence. Dramatic increase in personal power, certainty, and freedom from compulsive patterns.

IV

Ascension

The spirit begins operating independently of the reactive mind. Heightened perception, intuitive knowing, and the ability to influence the physical universe through pure spiritual intention.

V

Sovereignty

Complete mastery. The spirit exists in a state of total cause — fully self-determined, unrestricted, operating at capacities beyond ordinary human experience.

VI

Transcendence

The highest attainable state. Total spiritual freedom. The spirit operates with full awareness of its eternal nature and unlimited potential.

The Founder

Dr. Elias Voss

Founder & Spiritual Leader · b. 1943

Born in Vienna, Austria, to a family of academics, Elias Voss showed an extraordinary capacity for inquiry from early childhood. By the age of 19, he had studied across three universities and begun his lifelong investigation into the nature of consciousness.

His early academic career took him through neuroscience laboratories, quantum physics conferences, and the most rigorous halls of Western science. Yet it was a series of experiences in Eastern monasteries and indigenous healing traditions that catalyzed his breakthrough discovery: that beneath all systems of inner transformation — ancient and modern, Eastern and Western — lay a single, universal architecture.

In 1971, after two decades of research across 14 countries and hundreds of teachers, Dr. Voss codified this architecture into a complete and precise technology for the liberation of the human spirit. He called it Spiritology.

"I did not invent the technology of spiritual freedom. I discovered it — in the same way a physicist discovers a law of nature. It was always there. I simply mapped it."

Today, Dr. Voss continues to guide the Church from the International Headquarters in Clearfield, California. He has authored over 3,000 lectures, 40 books, and the complete technical volumes of The Bridge. His work has reached over 4.2 million people in 184 countries.
